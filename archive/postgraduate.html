Development of a BPMS system (Bonita): Radiology referral and procedure execution process using workflows

Jan 2022
Within a Health District it is possible to be referred by a specialist treating physician and to perform radiological procedures in hospitals. Each referring physician makes an appointment at the Hospital. The radiological operation of the referring person is scheduled (the date and time of execution is set) and the referring doctor and/or the patient are informed. When a radiological procedure is performed, the radiologist writes and sends to the patient's treating doctor a radiological report that includes the images and the radiological opinion
