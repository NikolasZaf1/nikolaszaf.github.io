Graduate Thesis for the development of a Diabetic Patient Management Information System (Angular + Django)

May 2021
The subject of this work is the examination of the Electronic Health Record (EHR) of a patient based on his medical history. The system that supports this reference and which was developed in the context of the thesis is the "ehealth" information system. With this system, the medical staff can directly and quickly see information of each patient's EHR. Both the modeling of the ICUs and the development of the models are done through the mechanisms of Artificial Intelligence (Artificial Intelligence - AI) to predict either what is the most appropriate treatment or whether he should be readmitted to the hospital. All mechanisms, as well as the user interface, are user-friendly through the use of new technologies to provide an appropriate management of electronic health records. Also, through the "ehealth" system, the doctor can immediately make an appointment for his patient, to register a patient, but also to see aggregated data. That is to see, both the analysis of the most important characteristics that it maintains in its file, as well as of all the characteristics that make up the electronic health record.
