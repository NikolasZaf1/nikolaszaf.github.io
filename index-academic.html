Research Interests

Health Data Interoperability

Leveraging standards such as FHIR to integrate healthcare data across systems for better patient care and data management.

Machine Learning in Healthcare

Developing predictive models for stroke, COVID-19, and other diseases using AI, contributing to improved healthcare outcomes.

Parkinson’s Disease Monitoring

Using graph neural networks and ontologies for personalized health monitoring, focusing on neurodegenerative diseases like Parkinson’s.

Wear4PDmove: Ontology for PD Monitoring

GAn ontology for knowledge-based personalized monitoring of Parkinson’s Disease patients.

Identity Management Standards

Researching identity management systems and standards for secure, efficient data access in digital health environments.

My goal is to harness these cutting-edge technologies to enhance patient care, streamline healthcare operations, and drive public health innovations.
